# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn & Promotion LTV Analysis

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project analyzes customer retention, churn rates, and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) across various promotional campaigns for a enterprise telecommunications provider. The goal is to identify high-risk customer segments and evaluate the long-term profitability of promotional strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Data Processing & Analytics:** SQL (CTEs, Conditional Aggregations, Window Functions)
* **Data Visualization:** Tableau Public (Interactive Dashboard)
* **Documentation & Insights:** Business Analysis & Strategy

---

## 🔍 Key Findings & Business Insights
1. **Promotion Risk:** Customers enrolled in short-term promotional upgrades experienced a **15% higher churn rate** compared to non-promotional long-term contracts.
2. **Contract Value:** Two-year contract customers yield the highest average LTV, generating **2.4x more revenue** over their lifecycle.
3. **Revenue at Risk:** Month-to-month contracts without technical support features represented over **60% of total monthly churned revenue**.
